Who are we?

Corporation, that was established in 2000, is a marble exportation and consultancy company. The major target of the company is to export the most quality marble products to around the world according to our customer's requests. Our customers prefer us in order to obtain their needs without losing any time, by relying on our quality customer service and trust-based marketing.

Shortly, by regularly visiting the marble pits around the world, we collect the most quality marble stones and quickly serve our customers based on their desires.

Block marbles, slabs, and specific sizes of marble products are being supplied by trustworthy service to around the world.

The fact that we check the marble stones that are produced in many marble pits makes us be informed about where are the most quality products and helps us so that we can serve in the best way to our customers.
